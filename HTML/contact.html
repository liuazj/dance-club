FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. When does Dance Club meet?
  2. Every day 2 and 4, twice a week (Monday and Wednesday or Tuesday and Thursday). When approaching performance dates, more practices may be added.
  3. Where does Dance Club meet?
  4. In Gym C, which can be accessed through the stairs next to the Gym hallway.
  5. Can I still join in the middle of the year?
  6. Of course you can! Just drop by Gym C any day we meet, and ask to be added to the email list.


If there are any other questions, don't be afraid to email us at colonelbydanceclub@gmail.com, or dm us @cb.dance on Instagram!
